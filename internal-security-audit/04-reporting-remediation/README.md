This module focuses on how audit findings are documented, reported to stakeholders, and translated into remediation tasks for accountability and tracking.

## Objectives

- Summarize key findings and risks identified during the fieldwork
- Assign ownership and due dates for remediation
- Document stakeholder communication
- Align findings with business priorities and compliance requirements

## Reporting Structure

- **Executive Summary:** High-level overview of findings, impact, and recommendations
- **Detailed Findings Table:** Control references, descriptions, evidence, severity, recommendations
- **Remediation Plan:** Owner, due date, action steps
- **Supporting Evidence:** Screenshots, logs, links to SOPs, etc.

## Stakeholder Engagement

- Review findings with IT, Security, and Governance teams
- Present report to business owners
- Determine final remediation owners and acceptance plans

## Tools

- Markdown reports
- Issue tracking tools (e.g., Jira, GitHub Issues, Asana)
- Audit binder for documentation
